# Introduction

## What is Aurellion?

Aurellions mission is to creating digital markets for physical assets.
While still enabilng true ownership of the physical asset.
Meaning redemption is us plaing the asset in your hands.

## What are we trying to solve?

Most physical commodities remain largely illiquid, no unified market or
infrastructure for instant trading and redemption

Pricing is inconsistent across regions and intermediaries. Leaving price discovery on the table.
Currently you can buy an ruby from one shop and sell it to another on the same street and get wildly different values.
Thats because the true spot price for emralds are obfusicated and fragmented keeping price discovery and fair value extremely low.

The end-to-end supply-chain data lacks visibility, eroding trust in
provenance.

Investors have no verifiable proof or physical access to the real assets
backing their investments.

## The Trade Financing Problem and how it unlocks yield.

At Aurellion we believe every a trade that makes money should be financed until it doesnt, where the point just before it doesnt is the trades fair value.

Commodity suppliers seeking capital to fund operations, are restricted to
traditional financing. Imagine you find a gold mine who's offering you 10% below spot but has order minimums of over $10mn. Now this is clearly an amazing deal but sourcing this amount of funds is typically the blocker for alot of suppliers realising the true price of an asset.
Aurellion seeks to solve this by consoldating capital via our native vaults or our vault partners in return for direct profit share on that deal.
That way Aurellion fractionalises access to these before impossible to reach markets.

The best part is that this is yield based on real economic activity and generative value!

## Our Solution

Aurellion tokenizes physical assets into verifiable digital twins.

Provable on-chain custody that can be physically redeemed at anytime.

Independent nodes verify each asset’s authenticity and custody status
before token minting

These tokenized asset than allow Aurellion to create markets and yield products.
